Percent of People of Color and educational inequality in Seattle Neighborhoods
Selected Neighborhood

Hover over a neighborhood

This smart dashboard maps the percent of people of color across Seattle neighborhoods (choropleth). To add context, the dashboard also shows two related neighborhood indicators from ACS: (1) percent of households with limited English, and (2) percent of residents with less than a high school education.

Hover over a neighborhood to see all three indicators and compare them in the bar chart. The map colors represent % People of Color, while the chart provides a quick multi-metric profile for the selected area. This project is intended to reveal the corelation between racial distribution and educational inequality.

Data Source: Seattle GeoData (Top 50 ACS Neighborhood Indicators)
